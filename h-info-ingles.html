    • SUBE

    To move with any type of transport around the city you only need a card GOES UP. - It is a unique modern, efficient and ecological electronic ticket system.-

    • How to obtain it?

      If you live or are visiting Buenos Aires, you can buy one card SUBE via the internet or at one of the SUBE obtaining centers. Then you must register it with your data on the internet and load it with money through home banking, at ATMs automatic, at authorized charging points (such as subway stations) or at terminals automatic SUBE.
    • In addition, if you are a visitor to the city, you can purchase your SUBE card at any of the Tourist Service Centers of the City. If you want to withdraw it, it is necessary to present a identity document or passport, an email and a telephone number to register the card.
